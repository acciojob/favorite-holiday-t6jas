Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India known for its beaches, nightlife, and vibrant culture. It’s a perfect place to relax and explore.

  1. Baga Beach – a popular spot for parties and water sports.
  2. Fort Aguada – a historic fort with a scenic ocean view.
  3. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – one of India’s tallest waterfalls.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market – a lively market with local crafts.
  • Fish Curry Rice – the staple Goan meal.
  • Prawn Balchão – a spicy seafood delicacy.
  • Bebinca – a traditional Goan dessert.
  • Sorpotel – a flavorful pork dish.
  • Feni – the local cashew liquor.
